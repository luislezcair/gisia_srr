National Iced Tea Day Deals from Starbucks and Teavana

\n\n*\n*\n*\n\nShare\n\n* Facebook\n* Twitter\n* Pinterest\n* LinkedIn\n* Instagram\n\nDownloads\n\nTo celebrate National Iced Tea Day, Starbucks and Teavana are offering customers three exclusive offers starting June 10 at participating specialty retail Teavana stores and Starbucks locations in the U.S.\n\nVisit Teavana Stores for a Chance to Win 365 Days of Teavana\n\nFor three consecutive weekends in June starting on June 10, customers can visit any specialty retail Teavana\u00ae store for the chance to win free tea for an entire year*, no purchase necessary.\n\n\u201cFree tea for a year at Teavana is the perfect way to experience some of our pure loose leaf teas and herbal infusions and try your hand at creating a custom blend as well,\u201d said Naoko Tsunoda, director, Global Tea Quality and Development. \u201cIt\u2019s a great way to build your tea expertise and take a stab at being a mixologist.\u201d\n\nThe Tea Good to Be True Instant Win game pieces will be distributed by partners (employees) in Teavana specialty retail stores the weekend of June 10-12, June 17-19 and June 24-26, while game piece supplies last. Ten customers, per distribution period, will win the grand prize of tea for a year, for a total of 30 Grand Prize winners. Every customer who receives an Instant Win game piece will benefit from a percentage off select Teavana teas and merchandise or a chance to win one 20 ounce iced tea**.\n\nAdditionally from June 9-12, customers can visit Teavana.com and get $25 off any $60 purchase plus free shipping, use the promo code BIGDEAL. Stock up on Teavana\u2019s exclusive tea blends to brew at home this summer.***\n\nOn June 10, Teavana stores will also be offering complimentary samples of iced tea including:\n\nRaspberry Limeade Tea Blend \u2013 A beautiful balanced myriad of berries with an elegant balsamic finish blended with a burst of lime.\n\nPineapple Berry Blue Tea Blend \u2013 Juicy, vibrant berry flavors blend beautifully with tropical sweet pineapple to create a flavorful and balanced blend that is perfect for iced tea. It\u2019s a blend of Blueberry Bliss and Pineapple Kona Pop that bursts with the tangy-sweet flavor of hibiscus, citrus and bold berry.\n\nGolden Monkey Strawberry Lemonade Tea \u2013 Inspired by Teavana\u2019s favorite tastes of summer, this invigorating blend is the perfect way to beat the heat. Black tea, strawberries and refreshing citrus combine to create a cannonball-sized splash of iced tea flavor.\n\n\u201cWe encourage customers to try different iced tea blends in our stores. You can explore different flavors, some you may not have considered on your own. This is the best way to uncover a new favorite tea,\" Tsunoda said. \"When you visit a Teavana store, spend time with one of our partners to learn about the range of tea flavors like berry, citrus, minty, dessert or spice profiles that appeal to your tastes,\"\n\nTea for Two at Starbucks Stores\n\nCustomers can share tea with a friend or family member at the Buy One, Share One Teavana Shaken Iced Tea Event at participating Starbucks\u00ae stores in the U.S. on June 10.\n\n\u201cWhether you\u2019re new to tea or a tea enthusiast, that first sip of delicious, loose leaf iced tea immediately transforms the way one thinks about this refreshing beverage,\u201d said Tsunoda. \u201cThe delicious, bold, exotic flavors are perfect for celebrating summer with family and friends.\u201d\n\nTsunoda recommends two tea beverages to try at Starbucks:\n\nTeavana\u00ae Shaken Iced Mango Black Tea Lemonade \u2013 A delicious blend of premium black tea, juicy mango, sweet passion fruit and a splash of lemonade.\n\nTeavana\u00ae Shaken Iced Peach Green Tea \u2013 Green tea, sweet peach, and ginger layered together for a taste that\u2019s peachy keen.\n\n*Free Tea for a Year means a 12-month subscription to Teavana\u00ae Tea of the Month Club (ARV $480).\n\n**Excludes Breville items or Teavana gift cards\n\n***Offer excludes Breville products, tea of the month club, and gift cards\n\nNO PURCHASE NECESSARY.
